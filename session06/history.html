Hooded warlocks

Hooded Warlocks possess a great deal of aura that is made known to anyone in their presence. They are masters of the dark arts and their hood compliments the nature of their dark magic. Although they do not match the traditional style of a wizard they have created their own class of wizards that still display their magical prowess. Hooded Warlocks are often seen pondering their orb and maintaining their immense magic power to uncover the dark secrets of the world. A foe dare not to interupt the warlock as he's pondering his awesome crystal orb, for it will be the last mistake they make.
